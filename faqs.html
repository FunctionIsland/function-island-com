Help & FAQs

What is D1VS?
D1VS is an hourglass smart contract, enabling anyone holding "D1VS" to earn TRX dividends, proportionate to supply shares held. The price and value of D1VS fluctuates at the mercy of holder actions - When holders sell their D1VS and withdraw TRX from the contract, the price and value of D1VS lowers proportionately. Inversely so, as users deposit TRX into the contract, the price and value both inflate proportionately.

This creates the effect of an "automated market", ensuring you can sell your D1VS at any time, and always receive something for them. With this in mind, it is perfectly possible to use D1VS as somewhat of a "savings vault" which is capable of earning passive TRX dividends, regardless of what other holders do!

How do I buy / sell D1VS?
To buy or sell D1VS, you will simply need to visit the Function Island website with a browser that has TronLink installed, or with a mobile Dapp browser like Klever Wallet, and go to the "My D1VS" tab. From there, you will be able to transact with the D1VS smart contract, so long as your wallet is unlocked, and you have enough TRX to cover transaction fees.

Why should I reinvest my dividends?
Reinvesting your Dividends means you earn more TRX dividends faster! Whenever you reinvest, your dividends are used to buy more D1VS for your bag, thus getting you a bigger portion of the supply. The more you do this, the faster you earn (or, that's at least the theory!)

Can I withdraw my Dividends?
Yes, you can always withdraw your Dividends at any time - however, it is recommended that you wait until you have at least 3 TRX in dividends before withdrawing, to make your transaction fee worthwhile!

I want to stop. How do I get out?
If you would no longer like to earn dividends by holding D1VS, simply follow the instructions to sell D1VS (all of them), and withdraw your TRX. Thank you!

Why doesn't D1VS show in my wallet, like other tokens?
D1VS is a special kind of token that may not show correctly with some wallets. So long as you can see your D1VS on the website, your D1VS are safe and ready to be transferred, sold or HODLed on further!

Does reinvesting also get a 10% fee?
The reinvest function of the D1VS contract calls the buy function, so yes - 10% of your TRX Dividends will be distributed to D1VS holders when you reinvest your dividends.

Can I profit from D1VS?
There is possibility of earning TRX from holding D1VS over time - however, keep in mind that D1VS is a game, not an investment platform. Only spend TRX you can afford to lose - D1VS value is at the mercy of the holders!
